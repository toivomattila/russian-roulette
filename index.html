How to Play Russian Roulette

Game Rules

  • The game uses a six-chamber revolver with one bullet
  • Click "Load & Spin Chamber" to randomly place the bullet
  • Click "Pull Trigger" to test your luck
  • If you survive, you can continue or restart
  • If you hit the loaded chamber, game over!

Probabilities

  • Initial chance of survival: 5/6 (83.33%)
  • Second shot: 4/5 (80%)
  • Third shot: 3/4 (75%)
  • Fourth shot: 2/3 (66.67%)
  • Fifth shot: 1/2 (50%)
  • Final shot: 0% chance of survival

Strategy

Each shot decreases your survival probability. Consider restarting after surviving a few shots to maintain better odds!
